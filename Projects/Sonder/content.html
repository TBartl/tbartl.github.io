Sonder (2016)

"As the world crumbles and is flooded with monsters, the only one you can trust is yourself. Scavenge broken cities for unique items that will let you withstand the horrors and other players. Only one can survive."

Sonder is a 3D multiplayer roguelike developed in Unity/C#. The goal of the project was to gain experience with larger projects and networking.

I worked on the project daily for 120 days, releasing an update video at the end of each month.

Download the game for Windows or Mac. Get the source code on git.
